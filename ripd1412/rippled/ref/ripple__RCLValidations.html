Description

Stores both listed and trusted validations. Listed validations are from nodes which are in a published lists, but which are not in this node's current trusted list. Trusted validations are validations received from nodes that are in the trusted list (UNL) at the time the validation arrives.

Implements the CRTP type requirements of Validations by saving stale validations to the sqlite DB.
